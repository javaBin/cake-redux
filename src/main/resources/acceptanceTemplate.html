OTHER PRACTICAL INFORMATION

1) Tickets:

All speakers receive a free ticket for the conference, which will be sent out in a later email. This includes the workshop day prior to the conference. Please note that if you wish to join one of the workshops as a participant, you have to register for the workshop just like any regular ticket holder would do.

2) Reimbursements:

Being a speaker at JavaZone does not automatically make you eligible for reimbursement. Instead, you have to apply for reimbursement. Please see https://2022.javazone.no/#/speakers/monetary-policy for information about how you can apply. Please note that we do not provide reimbursement to lightning-talk speakers.

3) Speakers dinner:

As always, we'll be hosting a speakers dinner the evening before JavaZone. Please indicate whether you'll attend or not when confirming your talk. You can expect more information about this in August.

For more information see: https://2022.javazone.no/#/speakers
