A NOTE ABOUT COVID

Most people in Norway are fully vaccinated, and most covid-related restrictions were recently lifted. The only restrictions that remain relate to international travel. However, if you can present proof of vaccination or undergone infection, there should be no problem.

Please note that if you need to travel internationally to reach the conference, you bear the responsibility to provide the requisite documentation for entry into Norway. Remember that there might be different rules that apply depending on your country of origin.

OTHER PRACTICAL INFORMATION

1) Tickets:

All speakers receive a free ticket for the conference, which will be sent out in a later email. This includes the workshop day prior to the conference. Please note that if you wish to join one of the workshops as a participant, you have to register for the workshop just like any regular ticket holder would do.

2) Reimbursements:

Being a speaker at JavaZone does not automatically make you eligible for reimbursement. Instead, you have to apply for reimbursement. Please see https://2021.javazone.no/#/speakers/monetary-policy for information about how you can apply. Please note that we do not provide reimbursement to lightning-talk speakers.

3) Speakers dinner:

As always, we'll be hosting a speakers dinner the evening before JavaZone. Please indicate whether you'll attend or not when confirming your talk. You can expect more information about this in November.

For more information see: https://2021.javazone.no/#/speakers
